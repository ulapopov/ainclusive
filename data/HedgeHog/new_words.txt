Spines: Hedgehogs have little pointy things on their backs, not sharp, to protect them.
Quills: Porcupines have very sharp, stick-out points that can hurt if you touch them.
Quilling: When baby hedgehogs change their small pointy things into grown-up ones.
Hedgehog: A small animal with pointy things on its back, but they're not too sharp. It rolls up into a ball when scared.
Porcupine: A bigger animal than a hedgehog, with very sharp points on its back that can stick into things.
